Featured statue

Christus Statue

The Christus is a white marble statue of the resurrected Jesus Christ by Danish sculptor Bertel Thorvaldsen. The original was completed in 1833 and is located in the Church of Our Lady in Copenhagen, Denmark. It has become especially familiar to Latter-day Saints through reproductions and visitor center displays.

This TNCM model was made using photos of the Christus statue in Rome, Italy, then shaped into a printable sculptural piece for reverent home display.
